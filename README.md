## What This Is

This isn't a tech blog. It's not a content strategy. There's no SEO plan, no posting schedule, no audience targeting.

This is where I write the things I actually think.

I'm a combat veteran, a paramedic, a father of five, and a software developer still waiting for the industry to open the door. I've been through war, burnout, identity collapse, career pivots, and the specific kind of silence that follows sending a job application into the void for the seventh month in a row.

Most people in tech write about frameworks and productivity hacks. I write about what it costs to get here — the hours, the missed holidays, the weight you carry home from a shift, the doubt that shows up at 2 a.m. when you're debugging and wondering if any of it is going to pay off.

No polish. No filters. Just the real thing.
